About me

I am a PhD candidate at the Department of Computer Science, Stony Brook University. I am co-advised by Michael Bender and Jing Chen. I am interested in the broad area of theoretical computer science; particularly in algorithms and data structures for computing on big data and game-theoretic study of complexity classes and rationality.

Before this, I pursued an Integrated MSc in Mathematics and Computing at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (2008 - 2013).
